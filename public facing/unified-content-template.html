TL;DR

Yes, opt in if you keep more than $250k in cash. The sweep program provides FDIC insurance on balances over $250k by automatically distributing funds across multiple banks.

What is the sweep program?

The sweep program is an automated service that distributes your cash balances across multiple FDIC-insured banks to maximize your deposit insurance coverage.

Here's why it matters: standard FDIC insurance only covers up to $250,000 per depositor, per bank. If you have more than that in cash, the excess isn't protected if the bank fails.

Watch: How the sweep program protects your cash (3:42)

How it works

  1. Automatic distribution
    When your cash balance exceeds $250,000, the sweep program automatically moves the excess to partner banks.
  2. Multiple bank coverage
    Each bank provides another $250,000 in FDIC insurance, so $1 million requires four banks.
  3. Seamless access
    You still see one balance and have full access to your funds—the distribution happens behind the scenes.
Quick demo: Enabling sweep in your account

Who needs it?

You should opt into the sweep program if you:

  • Regularly maintain cash balances over $250,000
  • Receive large deposits (property sales, business income, inheritance)
  • Want maximum protection without managing multiple bank relationships
Note: The sweep program is free for all Ethical Capital clients. There are no fees or minimum balance requirements.

Things to know

  • Interest rates: You'll earn the same rate across all swept funds
  • Tax reporting: You'll receive one consolidated 1099-INT
  • No delays: Transfers and withdrawals work exactly the same
Important: The sweep program only covers cash holdings. Your invested assets are protected by SIPC insurance up to $500,000, which is separate from FDIC coverage.

Common Questions

Does the sweep program affect my returns?
No. You earn the same interest rate whether your cash is swept or not. The only difference is the additional FDIC insurance coverage.
Can I see which banks hold my money?
Yes. Your account dashboard shows a breakdown of which banks hold your swept funds and how much is at each institution.
What if I need to withdraw more than $250k?
No problem. The system automatically aggregates funds from multiple banks when you make a withdrawal. There's no difference in how you access your money.

Next steps

Enabling the sweep program takes less than a minute:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Settings → Banking
  3. Toggle "Enable Sweep Program" to ON
  4. Review and accept the terms
That's it! The sweep program activates immediately and will automatically manage any cash balance over $250,000.
