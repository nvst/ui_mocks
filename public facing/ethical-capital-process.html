2

PROPRIETARY RESEARCH & EVALUATION

Beyond third-party ESG ratings, we evaluate companies through six fundamental lenses: People, Product, Execution, Valuation, Moat, and Risk. These criteria illustrate the kinds of things we look at—in practice, one might be operative while others aren't, and what matters changes over time. Our research combines quantitative analysis, lie detection technology, and multi-agent AI systems.

SIX FUNDAMENTAL EVALUATION LENSES

People

Do great people want to work here?

  • Employee satisfaction & retention
  • Leadership integrity & track record
  • Culture of innovation & excellence

Product

Do they make something people want?

  • Customer satisfaction & loyalty
  • Product-market fit evidence
  • Positive societal impact

Execution

Are they getting things done?

  • Operational efficiency metrics
  • Track record of meeting targets
  • Strategic initiative progress

Valuation

Can we buy at a reasonable price?

  • Price relative to intrinsic value
  • Growth prospects vs. multiples
  • Margin of safety assessment

Moat

Is their progress protected?

  • Competitive advantages
  • Barriers to entry
  • Network effects & switching costs

Risk

What could go wrong?

  • Regulatory & compliance risks
  • Market & competitive threats
  • Financial & operational vulnerabilities

We don't outsource ethics to rating agencies. 40% of our exclusions come from proprietary research that identifies issues others miss.

3

CONTINUOUS RELATIONSHIP & EVOLUTION

You should be able to actively consent to every position you hold. Life changes, and so should your portfolio. As I say to clients: "I could make you the best portfolio on the planet, but if it's losing you sleep at night, I've harmed you."

OUR ONGOING COMMITMENT:

  • Proactive Conversations: In every conversation, we proactively ensure your portfolio still aligns with your values and sleep patterns. While clients often reach out to us, we're always thinking about whether what you own still makes sense for who you are today.
  • Collaborative Intelligence: Your ethical concerns drive portfolio-wide policy updates. When you raise an issue, we research it and potentially update our screening for all portfolios. Every client makes us smarter.
  • Strategy Evolution: About 80% of our assets under management are in the Growth strategy—most of our clients are younger and it's a perfect fit. But we see client relationships as spanning a lifetime. As life changes, your strategy can too. We help you evolve at your pace.
  • Radical Transparency: Our exclusion criteria is open source—you can see exactly what we screen for and why. While specific company exclusion lists aren't public (legal reasons when you're calling out sketchy behavior), we maintain complete openness about our process and reasoning.
  • Continuous Monitoring: We monitor holdings continuously and rebalance systematically. I'm pretty sure nothing I have to say is more important than what's going on in your life—so I tend your garden regardless of whether you call, regularly reorienting your portfolio toward opportunity. Our screening criteria evolve based on client input, new research, and changing global conditions.

The result? A portfolio that grows with you, adapts to your changing life circumstances, and always respects your need for both financial returns and peaceful sleep. Your input doesn't just improve your portfolio—it makes our entire system better for everyone.
