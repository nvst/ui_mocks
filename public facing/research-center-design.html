The Hidden Costs of Greenwashing: A Quantitative Analysis of ESG Fund Performance

Our comprehensive study of 500+ ESG funds reveals surprising patterns in performance, fees, and actual environmental impact. This groundbreaking research challenges conventional wisdom about sustainable investing and provides a framework for identifying authentic impact opportunities.

Key Findings

  • 73% of "sustainable" funds hold fossil fuel investments
  • Average ESG fund fees are 0.42% higher than traditional funds
  • Only 18% of ESG funds outperformed benchmarks over 5 years
  • New framework for evaluating true impact potential
